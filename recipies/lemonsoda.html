Oreo Shake Recipe

Ingredients

  • Lemon: Juice of 1 fresh lemon (about 2 tablespoons)
  • 1.5 to 2 tablespoons (adjust to taste, or use simple syrup)
  • 1/4 teaspoon of table salt or black salt (kala namak) for an authentic Indian-style kick
  • Water: 2 tablespoons of chilled water
  • Club Soda: 1 cup (chilled)

Steps

  1. Mix the base: In a tall serving glass, combine the fresh lemon juice, sugar, salt, and chilled water. Stir vigorously until the sugar and salt are completely dissolved
  2. Add ice & aromatics: Drop in your ice cubes and mint leaves. (Pro-tip: Wet the ice cubes with a little water first—this prevents the soda from losing its fizz too quickly.)
  3. Pour the soda: Slowly pour the chilled club soda to fill the glass.
  4. Serve: Give it a gentle, quick stir and enjoy immediately while it is perfectly fizzy.
